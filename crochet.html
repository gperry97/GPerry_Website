Knit + Crochet Pieces

I have been crocheting and knitting for the last decade, starting when I was 12. I was initially taught by my aunt, who patiently would help me as I continued to mess up stitches and make lopsided scarves. Over the years I have improved, although I tend to have little patience for making large blankets or sweaters. I much prefer making animals or monsters since the pattern changes more from line to line, and I can complete them quicker. The dinosaurs photographed below are my most recent creations, and I can finish one in under 8 hours.

These pieces were made using a free or paid-for pattern, most with minor variations to accommodate small changes like size and yarn color patterns, etc. The monster patterns come from Rebecca Danger's Book of Knitted Monsters. The dinosaur pattern comes from an Etsy seller, Bluephone Studios.
