About

Gabriella Elyse Perry is pursuing a Master in Design Studies in Technology at the Harvard Graduate School of Design. She currently works at Virginia Tech's Center for Design Research as a Robotics and Additive Manufacturing Specialist. Her research focuses on non-layered large-format additive manufacturing. Her interests include digital fabrication and computational design in the realms of architecture, design, and fashion.

Gabriella graduated from Virginia Tech in May 2020 with a Bachelor of Architecture. During her undergraduate studies, she co-instructed multiple independent studies on additive manufacturing and managed the additive manufacturing and prototyping lab at the Center for Design Research.
