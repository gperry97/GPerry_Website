Harvard Univeristy Graduate School of Design, Enactive Design: Creative Applications Through Concurrent Human Machine Interaction, SCI-6365 Spring 2021

Ether is an online application for anxious amateurs experimenting with music composition. Ether eliminates the need for musical experience by removing the traditional idea of an instrument and allows users to compose collaboratively. The app exploits the serendipity of the internet by connecting people from across the world who are all trying to learn more about music composition, whilst also alleviating performance anxiety.
